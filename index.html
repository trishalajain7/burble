burble

Revolutionize the way you spend your time in the shower.

The Burble App allows users to listen to their favorite songs in the shower, while making a positive environmental impact. Users can track how much time they spend in the shower, view their progress, and connect with friends.

history

View your shower history to track your progress.

music

Burble generates a fun playlist containing 4 - 7.5 minutes of music. You have until the music stops to finish your shower.

leaderboard

See how you compare to the most environmentally-sustainable, music-loving shower-takers.
